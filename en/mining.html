Start CDOG mining & mining (take MyKey wallet for example)

First use half of your EOS to buy CDOG, and then use your remaining EOS and CDOG to do mining according to the following steps:

Open MyKey wallet, click "Browser"

Search for "DeFis", click to enter.

Click "Markets", find EOS/CDOG pair or USDT/CDOG pair. Click it, then click "Pools" below.

According to your chosen pair, enter the same proportion of EOS/CDOG or USDT/CDOG, then click "Join".

You can increase or withdraw your principal at any time. (You can also sell the DFS reward you get, Re-investment to increase your mining principal)
