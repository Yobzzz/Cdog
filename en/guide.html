Follow the steps to create a wallet, buy EOS for CDOG transactions (take MYKEY wallet as an example)

1. Download MyKey wallet

Click the button to visit the official website of MYKEY, and download MYKEY wallet according to the type of device you use.
Download

2. Register/Login, create EOS account。

2.1 Enter MyKey index, click「Register/Login」
2.2 After signing up, enter creating accounts, choose EOS for account type.
2.3 Set EOS account address. The system will generate an address randomly, or you can customize the address. Note: The length of the address must be 12 digits and consist of a-z, 1-5 numbers and letters.
2.4 Set the transaction password, pay attention to the prompt and remember the transaction password.
2.5 Successfully created your account.

3. Buy EOS on the exchange and withdraw the coins to the EOS address of MyKey wallet。

Take Huobi APP for example here. If you know how to withdraw coins from your familiar exchange, ignore this.
3.1 Sign in Huobi APP, click "Buy Crypto" Huobi Exchange
3.2 Purchase EOS according to your own payment method and conduct fiat currency transactions.
3.3 After the fiat currency transaction is completed, withdraw EOS to the MyKey address.
3.4 Click "Withdraw", wait for transaction completing.

4. Buy CDOG

4.1 Open MyKey wallet, click "Browser".
4.2 Search for "DeFis", click to enter.
4.3 On "Market" page, click "EOS" on nav, find the CDOG/EOS trading pair, use EOS to buy CDOG.
